Tofu Masala

Description

This version of Indiaâ€™s famed fiery orange curry is made with tofu for a flavorful vegan main dish that can be served on its own or with rice or naan.

Ingredients

  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (14 ounce) package extra-firm tofu, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 3/4 cup coconut milk
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (1 inch) piece fresh ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons madras curry powder, or more to taste
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. Stir tomatoes, tofu, onion, coconut milk, garlic, ginger, tomato paste, curry powder and salt together in a large stock pot over medium heat; bring to a boil.
  2. Cover pot with a lid, reduce heat to medium-low, and cook at a simmer until aromatic and the tofu has absorbed the curry flavor, 15 to 25 minutes.
  3. Divide curry between 4 bowls; top fresh cilantro to serve.
